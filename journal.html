Journal Introduction

    "Proceeding of Japan-China Joint Symposium on Computational Material Sciences and Monte Carlo Methods" is a pioneering English-language journal that serves as a nexus for the latest advancements in the fields of Computational Material Sciences and Monte Carlo Methods, particularly focusing on the collaborative efforts and shared insights between Japan and China. Established with a vision to bridge the research communities of these two leading nations in science and technology, the journal offers a unique platform for scholars, researchers, and practitioners to explore and exchange novel ideas, breakthroughs, and applications in these dynamic and increasingly important areas of study.

Instructions to Authors

    Thank you for your interest in Proceeding of Japan-China Joint Symposium on Computational Material Sciences and Monte Carlo Methods, a dynamic platform committed to sharing groundbreaking and influential research. We are delighted at the prospect of you contributing your research to our publication. To aid in your submission and to facilitate an effective and streamlined review process, we have prepared an extensive guide, entitled "Instructions to Authors." This resource outlines thorough instructions on the preparation, formatting, and submission of manuscripts, as well as insights into our peer-review procedure. We highly recommend that all potential authors review this guide meticulously to ensure compliance with our publication standards. This attention to detail will significantly enhance the review process of your submission. We appreciate your dedication to enriching the academic dialogue in our community.

Copyright Transfer Agreement

    In order to complete the publication process and enable the distribution of your research via Proceeding of Japan-China Joint Symposium on Computational Material Sciences and Monte Carlo Methods, you need to sign and return the attached Copyright Transfer Agreement. This agreement, along with your submitted manuscript, is a prerequisite for your work to be eligible for publication consideration. In the event that your submission is not chosen for publication in our journal, the conditions of this Agreement will no longer apply.
